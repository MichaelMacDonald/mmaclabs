Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

MMac Labs ("Company", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our software applications and services ("Services").

1. Information We Access

Our Services integrate with third-party platforms (such as QuickBooks Online and payment processors) on your behalf. When you authorize a connection, our Services may access:

  • Financial transaction data (invoices, bills, payments, bank transactions)
  • Account and company information
  • Chart of accounts and categorization data
  • Contact information for customers and vendors within your accounting platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We access your data solely to provide the Services you have requested, including:

  • Retrieving and displaying financial data for review
  • Categorizing and organizing transactions
  • Generating financial reports
  • Automating bookkeeping workflows

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Services.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Our Services operate primarily as client-side tools. Data retrieved from third-party platforms is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain a central database of user financial data.
  • OAuth tokens and credentials are stored locally on your device in encrypted or access-restricted files.
  • We do not retain your financial data after the requested operation is complete.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal or financial data with third parties. Your data is transmitted only between your device and the authorized third-party platform (e.g., QuickBooks Online) using secure, encrypted connections.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Using OAuth 2.0 for secure authorization with third-party platforms
  • Storing credentials with restricted file permissions
  • Transmitting data over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections

No system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and credentials.

6. Your Rights

  • You may disconnect our Services from your third-party accounts at any time through the platform's authorization settings.
  • You may request deletion of any locally stored credentials by removing the application configuration files.
  • Disconnecting revokes our access immediately — no residual data remains on our systems.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Services interact with third-party platforms governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any connected platforms (e.g., Intuit's privacy policy for QuickBooks Online).

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at mike@mmaclabs.com.
