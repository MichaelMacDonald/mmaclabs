Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled by the internal software tools developed by Michael Mac Donald under MMac Labs ("Developer", "I", "me"). These tools are built for the Developer's personal and internal business use and are not offered as a service to third parties.

1. Nature of the Application

MMac Labs tools are private, internal-use applications that integrate with third-party platforms (such as QuickBooks Online) via their official APIs. They are used exclusively to manage the Developer's own business accounts. No third-party users connect their data through these tools.

2. Data Accessed

When connected to a third-party platform, these tools may access:

  • Financial transaction data (invoices, bills, payments, bank transactions)
  • Account and company information
  • Chart of accounts and categorization data
  • Contact information for customers and vendors
  • Financial reports (profit and loss, balance sheet, etc.)

All data accessed belongs to the Developer's own business accounts.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is accessed solely for the purpose of:

  • Reviewing and categorizing financial transactions
  • Generating financial reports for internal use
  • Automating routine bookkeeping tasks
  • Reconciling bank feeds and account balances
  • Preparing data for the Developer's accountant and tax submissions

Data is never used for advertising, profiling, analytics on third parties, or any purpose unrelated to the Developer's own business operations.

4. Data Storage and Processing

  • All data processing occurs locally on the Developer's own devices.
  • No central server or cloud database stores business or financial data.
  • OAuth 2.0 tokens and API credentials are stored locally in access-restricted files (owner-only permissions).
  • No financial data is retained beyond the immediate operation being performed.

5. Data Sharing

The Developer does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share business or financial data with any third party. Data is transmitted only between the Developer's device and the authorized third-party platform (e.g., QuickBooks Online) using secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

6. Security Measures

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authorization with third-party platforms
  • All API communication over HTTPS/TLS encrypted connections
  • Credentials and tokens stored with restricted file permissions (chmod 600)
  • Client secrets are never hardcoded in source code or exposed in application logs
  • Access tokens are refreshed only when expired, per platform guidelines

7. Third-Party Platform Compliance

These tools interact with third-party platforms governed by their own privacy policies and terms of service. The Developer complies with all applicable platform requirements, including Intuit's Developer Terms of Service and Global Privacy Statement.

8. No Collection of Third-Party Personal Data

These tools do not collect, process, or store personal data from third-party individuals. Any customer or vendor contact information within the Developer's accounting platform is the Developer's own business data, managed in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

OAuth tokens may be stored locally for the duration of the authorized connection. Revoking access through the third-party platform (e.g., disconnecting the app in QuickBooks Online) immediately terminates the tool's access. Locally stored tokens can be deleted by removing the application configuration files.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
